# CEO / Partner Persona — Lawyer's Perspective

## Persona

Two partners running a boutique law firm in Plovdiv. They care about credibility, trust, and converting visitors into clients. The website is their primary digital storefront.

## Requirements

### 1. Credibility & Authority
- Every lawyer must have: full name, title, practice areas, years of experience, bar association membership
- Bios must sound professional but approachable — no fluff, no broken Bulgarian
- Photos are deliberately absent (abstract visuals only) — design must compensate with typography, layout, and tone
- Trust signals: bar membership, years in practice (2010/2012), firm founding year (2016), Kapana location

### 2. Practice Area Presentation
- All 8 areas must be listed with clear, accurate descriptions
- Grouped logically (Public / Private / Business) — makes the firm look organised
- Each card must answer: "What does this mean for me?" not just a textbook definition

### 3. Contact Accessibility
- Phone must be tappable (tel:) and visible in header on every page
- Email(s) present and clickable
- Address must be complete with neighbourhood (Kapana), floor, office number
- Working hours clear

### 4. Visual & Professional Tone
- Colour must feel serious but warm — no bright or playful palettes
- Font must be clean, readable (Inter is fine)
- No stock photos of fake lawyers, gavels, or justice scales
- No English text anywhere — Bulgarian only
- No emojis, exclamation marks in body text, or casual language

### 5. Mobile & Performance
- Must work perfectly on a phone (clients browse from phones)
- Fast load — under 2s on 3G
- No broken layouts at any width
